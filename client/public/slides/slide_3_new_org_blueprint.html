The New Organizational Blueprint

Agent Boss
Every employee becomes a manager of digital labor, requiring new skills in delegation and oversight.
Human-Agent Ratio
A new metric optimizing the balance between human oversight and agent efficiency.
The Work Chart
Replaces the org chart. Structured around "jobs to be done" rather than functional silos.
Intelligence Resources
A new function blending IT and HR to manage digital labor at an organizational level.
Strategic Imperative
Leaders must redesign business processes now to harness the increasing capabilities of AI agents.
