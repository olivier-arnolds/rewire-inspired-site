The Transformation Gap

Only 3% of companies have truly transformed with AI. While executives say AI has made significant impact, employees experience it as experimental and fragmented.

Executive Optimism vs. Employee Reality

Leaders are more likely to claim AI success, but employees are held back by lack of top-down strategy, robust training, and clear AI roadmap.

Stuck in Experimentation

Most organizations remain in pilot mode with standalone AI experiments rather than integrated, transformative deployments across workflows.
