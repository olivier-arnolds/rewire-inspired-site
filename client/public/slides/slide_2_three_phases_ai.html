Key message - Takeaways

The Three Phases of AI Transformation

Phase 1
AI as Assistant
AI removes drudgery, helping employees do existing work better and faster (e.g., drafting emails, summarizing meetings).
Phase 2
Agents as Digital Colleagues
Agents join teams to perform specific tasks under human direction (e.g., creating plans), freeing humans for higher-value work.
Phase 3
Agent-Led Workflows
Humans set direction for agents that run entire business processes autonomously, intervening only for exceptions.
Key Insight
Just as software development evolved from coding assistance to autonomous agents, knowledge work is undergoing a profound structural change.
