# 2025: The Year the Frontier Firm is Born

## Slide 1: The Rise of the Frontier Firm
- **Concept:** A new organizational model is emerging, defined by "intelligence on tap" and human-agent collaboration.
- **Key Driver:** The "Capacity Gap"—business demands are outpacing human capacity, with 80% of the workforce lacking time/energy and 53% of leaders demanding higher productivity.
- **Solution:** AI agents as "digital labor" to scale workforce capacity on demand.
- **Adoption:** 82% of leaders see 2025 as a pivotal year for strategy; 81% expect to integrate agents extensively in the next 12-18 months.
- **Frontier Firms:** Early adopters (Frontier Firms) are already thriving, with 71% of their leaders reporting success vs. 39% globally.

## Slide 2: The Three Phases of AI Transformation
- **Image:** /home/ubuntu/rewire-inspired-site/client/public/images/slides/ai-transformation-phases.png
- **Phase 1: AI as Assistant:** AI removes drudgery, helping employees do existing work better and faster (e.g., drafting emails, summarizing meetings).
- **Phase 2: Agents as Digital Colleagues:** Agents join teams to perform specific tasks under human direction (e.g., a researcher agent creating a plan), freeing humans for higher-value work.
- **Phase 3: Agent-Led Workflows:** Humans set direction for agents that run entire business processes autonomously, intervening only for exceptions or oversight.
- **Evolution:** Similar to software development's shift from coding assistance to autonomous agents, knowledge work is undergoing a profound structural change.

## Slide 3: The New Organizational Blueprint
- **Image:** /home/ubuntu/rewire-inspired-site/client/public/images/slides/new-org-blueprint.png
- **New Roles:** "Agent Boss"—every employee becomes a manager of digital labor, requiring new skills in delegation and oversight.
- **New Metrics:** "Human-Agent Ratio"—optimizing the balance between human oversight and agent efficiency.
- **New Structure:** The "Work Chart" replaces the org chart, organizing around "jobs to be done" rather than functional silos.
- **Intelligence Resources:** A new function blending IT and HR to manage digital labor at an organizational level.
- **Call to Action:** Leaders must redesign business processes now to harness the increasing capabilities of AI agents.
